## Source

  Pixar Spine) — see dossier.
- Why now: add a literature-grounded writing capability to the catalog, in the
  repo's `*-audit`/`*-design` pair formula; lens = **work-writing-first**.

## Recommended Pack Decisions

- **Pack tag(s):** `writing`, plus cross-cutting tags (`editing`, `prose`,
  `rhetoric`, `structure`, `persuasion`, `storytelling`, `revision`,
  `copyediting`, `clarity`, `communication`).
- **New family/pack?** Yes — propose a new catalog **family `communication`**
  (`metadata.family`), with `function: design` and `function: audit`. Justified
  per `pack-placement-rubric.md`: (1) names a capability domain, not a source;
  (2) survives if any book disappears; (3) two candidates cluster now and the
  family can accept future sources (speaking, naming, messaging); (4) keeps the
  `heuristics` family coherent as *software/product* craft.
  **Alternative considered:** fold into `heuristics` (defensible — same
  "literature-grounded audit/design" method). Recommend `communication`;
  **this is the one open decision for the gate.**
